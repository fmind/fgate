# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions, issues, and reviews.
- Give and accept feedback gracefully. Critique ideas, not people.
- Assume good faith. Ask questions before reaching conclusions.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, public or private.
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that don't align with this Code of Conduct, and may ban contributors for behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

Report concerns to <github@fmind.dev>. Reports will be reviewed and acted on promptly and confidentially.
